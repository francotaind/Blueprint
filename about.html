About Blueprint Construction

Our Company

Blueprint Construction and Engineering Limited (BCEL) is a building and civil works contractor incorporated in 2015 and officially registered in 2022 under the Companies Act Cap 486 of Kenya. The company aims to provide efficient, reliable, and competitively priced construction and civil works services.

Run by experienced builders with strong backgrounds in construction, civil engineering, and finance, BCEL focuses on delivering quality projects on time, ensuring client satisfaction, and providing value for money.

Our Mission

To procure projects at competitive pricing, ensure safe working conditions, deliver quality within reasonable time, and invest in assets and technology.

Our Vision

To deliver high-quality projects that meet international standards, using innovative solutions with integrity, while contributing to national infrastructure development.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We conduct our business with honesty and transparency.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in all our projects.

Customer Satisfaction

Our clients' needs are at the heart of everything we do.
